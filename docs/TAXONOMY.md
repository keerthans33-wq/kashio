# Taxonomy

Deduction categories used in Kashio for the MVP.
These map to common ATO work-related expense categories for Australian employees.

---

## Categories

### 1. Donations

**What it is:** Money given to an approved charity or not-for-profit with no benefit received in return.

**Examples:**
- Donations to the Red Cross, Cancer Council, or other DGR-registered charities
- Workplace giving contributions

**User must confirm:**
- The organisation is DGR (Deductible Gift Recipient) registered
- They received nothing in return (not a raffle ticket, merchandise, etc.)
- The amount is $2 or more

**Evidence needed:**
- Receipt or email confirmation from the charity
- Donation amount and date

---

### 2. Work Software & Tools

**What it is:** Subscriptions or purchases of software and digital tools used for work.

**Examples:**
- Microsoft 365, Adobe Creative Cloud, Notion, Slack
- App purchases used for work tasks
- Domain names or hosting if required for work

**User must confirm:**
- The software is used for work, not personal use
- If used for both, only the work-use portion can be claimed

**Evidence needed:**
- Receipt or invoice from the provider
- Subscription confirmation email

---

### 3. Office Supplies & Equipment

**What it is:** Items bought to do your job, used in a home office or at work.

**Examples:**
- Pens, notebooks, printer paper, folders
- A keyboard, mouse, monitor, or webcam
- A desk or chair (if used for work)

**User must confirm:**
- The item is used for work
- If used for both work and personal use, only the work portion can be claimed
- Items over $300 may need to be depreciated rather than claimed in full

**Evidence needed:**
- Receipt or invoice showing item name, date, and amount

---

### 4. Tax Affairs Expenses

**What it is:** Costs related to managing your own tax obligations.

**Examples:**
- Tax agent or accountant fees
- Purchase of tax software (e.g. H&R Block, etax)
- Postage or travel to visit your tax agent

**User must confirm:**
- The expense relates to their personal tax return, not a business

**Evidence needed:**
- Invoice or receipt from the tax agent or software provider

---

### 5. Working From Home

**What it is:** A portion of home running costs attributed to working from home.

**Examples:**
- Internet bill (work-use portion)
- Electricity and gas (work-use portion)
- Phone bill (work-use portion)

**Calculation method (ATO fixed rate):**
- 70 cents per hour worked from home
- Users must log their WFH hours in Kashio

**User must confirm:**
- They worked from home on the days logged
- They are not already reimbursed by their employer

**Evidence needed:**
- A record of hours worked from home (provided by Kashio's WFH log)
- At least one bill for each expense type (internet, electricity, etc.)
